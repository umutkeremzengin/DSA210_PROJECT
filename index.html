Digital Life vs Physical Activity

Analyzing the Relationship Between Social Media Engagement and Daily Steps

Project Overview

This analysis explores the intricate relationship between physical activity tracked through daily step counts and social media engagement metrics from Instagram and TikTok. By examining these patterns over time, we aim to uncover potential correlations between digital behavior and physical activity.

Research Questions

  • Primary Question: How does daily step count correlate with social media engagement levels?
  • Secondary Analysis: Are there specific patterns in social media activity on high vs. low step count days?
  • Time Patterns: Do weekday vs. weekend patterns show different relationships between steps and engagement?

Data Sources

Step Count Data

Daily physical activity metrics collected through health tracking

Instagram Metrics

Post engagement data including likes and interaction times

TikTok Analytics

Video performance and engagement statistics

Methodology

  • Data collection from health tracking and social media platforms
  • Statistical analysis of correlations between variables
  • Time series analysis for pattern identification
  • Visualization of relationships and trends
